Carne Asada taco

Description

The meat just falls apart and is so tender. It's packed with so much flavor!

Ingredients

  • Flank Steak
  • Onion
  • Vegetable Oils
  • Limes
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the steak
  2. Grill over medium high heat
  3. Dice and add half the onions to skillet
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Melt butter
  6. Fry mini tortitllas
  7. Assemble and enjoy!
