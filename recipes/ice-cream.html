Creamy Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Cold, sweet, and creamy! A delicious treat for any day!

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Whole milk
  • Sugar
  • Fine sea salt
  • Vanilla extact or beans
  • Soft Brownies (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour cup of cream into saucepan
  2. Add sugar and salt
  3. Scrape seeds of vanilla bean into pot
  4. Mix mixture over medium heat
  5. Remove from heat and add remaining cream, milk, and extract
  6. Churn and mix
  7. Remove and enjoy!
