Lasagna

Description

Delicious, meaty, and cheesy, this lasagna recipe is guaranteed to give you euphoria!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Sugar

Steps

  2. Finish the sauce
  3. Make the cheese filling
  4. Preheat oven and cook noodles
  5. Assemble meat sauce, noodles, and cheese mix
